Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is a simple yet delicious recipe for scrambled eggs. This recipe contains a small amount of ingredients that are both easy to find as well as cheap. All in all, this recipe should take no longer than 15 minutes and can feed between 2 and 4 people.

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter

Steps

  1. lightly beat eggs, along with three quarters of a teaspoon of salt, and a pinch of black pepper
  2. Melt 1 tablespoon of butter in a medium non-stick skillet, coating bottom and sides of pan.
  3. Add eggs to pan, and scrape them occasionally with a rubber spatula, until eggs are soft.
  4. Remove eggs, and add to plate.
