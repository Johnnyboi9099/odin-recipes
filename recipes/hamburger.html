Hamburger

Description

This is a simple recipe for a hamburger, made to include a small list of ingredients that are both easy to find and cheap. This recipe should take no longer than 35 minutes and will create some of the best hamburgers that you have ever had. Grill required.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill to high eat and lightly oil surface
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together an egg with salt and pepper
  3. place ground beef and bread crubs into mixture
  4. mix until well blended. Form 4 patties, with an equal amount of beef
  5. Place patties on grill and cook for 6-8 minutes per side
